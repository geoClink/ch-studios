CommonSight

Client — We Change Community Consulting

iOS · SwiftUI · Supabase

Overview

CommonSight is a civic tech platform designed to connect Detroit residents and investors looking to improve neighborhoods. The app expedites the process of community development, allowing more people to get involved who otherwise wouldn't have access, while helping save the city money.

The Problem

Detroit neighborhood improvement has historically been slow and inaccessible. Residents who want to see change in their communities have no direct way to connect with investors, and investors have no efficient way to identify where their resources are most needed.

The Solution

We built CommonSight to bridge that gap — a mobile platform that puts residents and investors in the same room digitally. The app surfaces neighborhood data, connects stakeholders, and streamlines a process that previously required significant time and city resources.

Our Role

CH Studios was contracted by We Change Community Consulting to lead development of the CommonSight iOS application. We owned the full mobile development lifecycle — from architecture and design through to implementation and testing.

Tech Stack

  • iOS & SwiftUI — native mobile frontend
  • Supabase — backend and database

Outcome

Currently in active development under contract with We Change Community Consulting. Building toward a public launch serving Detroit residents and community investors.
