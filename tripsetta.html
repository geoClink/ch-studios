Overview

Tripsetta's founder built all three versions of the app (iOS, Android, and web) solo. As the product grew, keeping up with maintenance, bug fixes, and user-driven improvements across three platforms became unmanageable for one person. CH Studios was brought on as the engineering partner to take over ongoing maintenance and ensure the product could continue to evolve.

Our Role

We work directly with the owner, translating user feedback into concrete improvements across all three platforms. Our day-to-day work includes diagnosing and patching bugs reported by real users, shipping incremental improvements, and keeping the codebase stable as the product matures.

What We Handle

  • Bug Fixes: Identify and resolve issues surfaced by real user reports across iOS, Android, and web.
  • User-Driven Updates: Translate owner and user feedback into targeted improvements, shipped consistently across all platforms.
  • Cross-Platform Maintenance: Keep three separate codebases stable and in sync as the product evolves.
  • Ongoing Partnership: Act as a long-term engineering resource so the founder can focus on product direction rather than code.
