About CH Studios

A Detroit-based software studio crafting mobile apps and full stack products.

We were both new to coding when we met at the Apple Developer Academy. We got randomly assigned to a project together, worked well, and just kept choosing to work with each other. When a client needed an LLC to pay us for services, we stopped being classmates and became a studio. That was the moment CH Studios was born — not from a business plan, but from good work and the right opportunity.
